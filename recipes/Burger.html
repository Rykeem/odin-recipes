The Best Burger

Lets go back to one of the classics. Who doesn't love a good burger now and then. With almost unlimited toppings nothing can go wrong.

Ingredients

  • Burger Patty
  • Topping
  • Hamburger bun
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Garlic Powder

Where to begin

  1. Get a mixing bowl add your patty, egg, milk, sauce and spices. And combine mixture until it is very smooth
  2. Preheat grill to 350 - 400 Degrees F
  3. Cook patties for 3-4 minutes on each side
  4. Stack your burger on the buns
  5. Add your toppings
  6. Boom All done enjoy ;)
